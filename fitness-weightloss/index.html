Weight loss

How to lose weight with my help

Beauty is in the eye of the beholder. To me, that means we don’t need to conform to a mass approach to what shape our bodies should be in or adopt the herd-like mentality we are force fed by the fashion industry. Big and beautiful or skinny and sexy. Doesn’t matter. It’s more about how you feel about yourself.

That said, we cannot ignore the global phenomenon of obesity. Mass production of food, the globalisation of our food supply and an increasingly more sedentary lifestyle means being physically healthy today is no small challenge.

Good weight management isn’t just about people how much people eat combined with not eating ‘bad’ foods and doing a bit of exercise every day. There is so much to understand about the way our bodies work. No doubt nutrition and exercise are two sides of the same coin in maintaining optimal physical health. Yet a lack of real understanding of this means more and more people are suffering from preventable illnesses as a direct result of what they eat.

So whether you want to lose a bit of weight, need a physical overhaul or you want to attain peak fitness like an athlete, we should talk. Get in touch – I would love to help you achieve your health and fitness goals!
